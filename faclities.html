ABOUT THE FACLITIES


The institute has an infirmary on campus to meet with the first aid and routine medical requirements of the students free of cost. In the same health care centre, consultation is also provided to the members of faculty and staff of the institute. The infirmary is well – resourced with basic medical facilities as per prescription of a registered medical clinician and a registered psychologist.

Today, the global job scenario demands much more than a mere acquaintance with the English language. The demands are rather high for the professionals with a correct sense of use of the language as a medium of effective communication. Confidence in speech and self, stress handling, sense of cohesion, presentation skills and above all a solid information pool to back up one's ideas with – all concretize together to mould a student into an employable communicator. The state of the art language Lab is designed to facilitate an overall development of our students in Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, under the adroit guidance of our faculties of English Language & Communication. Apart from following the syllabus of Maulana Abdul Kalam University of Technology, we have specially designed Soft Skill Development programs to develop our students' communicative skills, inculcating a sense of Corporate and social responsibility in them.

The Central Library -a proud partner in the institute's march towards its vision, plays a vital role in acquisition, organization, and dissemination of knowledge. It has put in place policies and procedures, systems and services, and an ambience to facilitate acquisition of knowledge.

     Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology’s Central Library is well-stocked, containing all the latest editions of the publications related to the curriculum; enabling the students to enhance their knowledge on different topics and subjects incorporated in their courses. The latest periodicals, journals and other publications are also provided for constant updation in the field of Science and Technology.
