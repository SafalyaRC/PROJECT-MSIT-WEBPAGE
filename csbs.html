Computer Science and Business System


CSBS is a new and a revolution in the field of engineering. The course is designed so that skilled engineers not only gain knowledge in Computer Science but also get an exposure in business system, which make them stand out from the crowd of CSE students. If they choose to do higher studies in MBA, you will have an upper hand than the other engineering students. As always, it's extremely beneficial to have completed relevant work experience.
Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology (MSIT) in collaboration with India’s leading Company – Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is offering a four year specialized B.Tech course titled “Computer Science and Business Systems” (CSBS) in the year 2020 to cater to the rising need of engineering talent with skills in technology. This course aims to impart knowledge of cutting-edge technologies and business skills with hands-on exposure to make students industry-ready.


1.Programme Type: Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
2.Course collaboration with: Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)
3.Duration: 4 years
4.Entry through: Joint Entrance Examination (JEE)
5.Approved by: All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
6.Affiliated to: Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT)
7.Course Developed by: Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)
8.Number of seats: 30 per year


Salient features of CSBS:


The syllabus is prepared based more on skills than theory which most of the big MNCs require. So that skillful engineers will be produced and not bookworms, which will make you stand out from the crowd Business-related topics like humanities, management science were added instead of other topics which would help students to enhance their skills in multiple fields The course is designed so that skilled engineers and not bookworms are produced, which make you stand out from the crowd of CSE students.
