Civil Engineering


The Department of Civil Engineering, MSIT is one of the most reputed civil engineering departments of West Bengal and the oldest Civil Engineering department among the private Engineering Colleges under the affiliation of West Bengal University of Technology(WBUT) presently known as Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT). The undergraduate Civil Engineering degree program is running successfully since 2003. The courses are approved by the Department of Higher Education, Govt. of West Bengal and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Ministry of Human Resource development, Govt. of India.


The Civil Engineering department was also a study center of Indira Gandhi Nation Open University (IGNOU) for Bachelor degree in construction management and water recourse engineering in West Bengal from 2007 to 2013. The department has produced highly qualified civil engineering professionals and continues this legacy keeping pace with rapidly changing technologies and global needs. The department is also offering postgraduate engineering degree program on Geotechnical Engineering from the year 2011. The present student intake of the department for the under graduate program is 120 and for the post graduate program it is 18. The department has well experienced faculty. The students from different parts of the country are studying in the department. The department has state of the art laboratories for Geotechnical Engineering, Concrete and material testing, Environmental engineering, Transportation engineering, Fluid mechanics, Solid Mechanics, Geology, and Survey practice. Computer Laboratory is equipped with latest civil engineering softwares like Auto CAD, STAAD PRO etc. The department also has its own library housing a good number of quality reference books for the benefit of students and staff.
